RUNNER/GRIDLOCK is a real-time action rogue-like. You control a RUNNER and you must drift, dodge, and steal your way through an abstract procedural city.

Too late, you notice the tell-tale glint of a laser designator dancing across your visual sensors as you cross a crowded intersection at high speed. They've got a lock on you. You drift hard to the north. Hopefully you can break their lock with a detour through the alley ahead and still make it to the exit with your package intact.

CONTROLS

  • Tap WASD to move.
  • Hold WASD to accelerate.
  • Hold SHIFT to engage turbo (when at top speed)
  • Hold SPACE to brake
  • Press B to reset.

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Seek out a green vehicle with a DATA CORE to steal. One is active at a time; check your minimap to see where. Drive along-side it to complete the objective.
  2. Avoid the COPTER. It will quickly shred your RUNNER.
  3. COPTER will seek you if any enemy has a lock on your at the moment.
  4. Fill the objective meter to unlock an exit.
  5. Break all locks to regenerate integrity.
